## 👋 About Us

**Al-Germaniyya IT Group** is a team of IT professionals focused on building secure, scalable, and reliable technology solutions.

We support Al-Germaniyya through infrastructure management, networking, cybersecurity, software development, automation, virtualization, monitoring, and digital transformation.

We are also ready to collaborate professionally with external organizations, including nonprofits, churches, healthcare providers, educational institutions, and other mission-driven organizations.
